# App Store Review Notes

DVNT includes event discovery, ticketing, social posting, messaging, and live video rooms.

## Demo Account

Use the seeded Apple review account when testing gated flows:

- Email: `applereview@dvntapp.live`
- Password: provided in App Store Connect review notes

Suggested review path:

1. Sign in.
2. Open Messages.
3. Tap Private Lynk.
4. Create or join a Private Lynk.
5. Open the room controls to verify Report is available.
6. Open the participant list as host to verify moderation controls are available.

## Private Lynk

Private Lynk is the temporary App Store review name for DVNT's live audio/video rooms. Hosts can remove participants, all users have an in-room Report button, and reports are stored for safety review. Screen capture protection is used to protect participant privacy, not to evade accountability. Rooms are live-only and DVNT does not record room audio or video.

## Account Deletion

Account deletion is available in-app from Settings. The deletion flow cancels subscriptions, refunds active paid tickets before voiding remaining tickets, ends hosted live rooms, deregisters push/VoIP tokens, revokes Apple Sign In tokens when present, deletes Better Auth sessions/accounts, and anonymizes financial records needed for bookkeeping.

## Sign In With Apple

DVNT offers Sign in with Apple anywhere third-party or social login is offered.
